About Nelieca

Nelieca is a platform for people to come together and share their thoughts and ideas, thereby building their dreams. It is an edtech platform that identifies course institutions, jobs, events, news, and more. Nelieca will be available on both web and app. Nelieca's features are designed for the development of the student and start-up communities. The purpose of Nelieca is to build skills and support each other. Start-ups and educational platforms can introduce their products and services on Nelieca. The benefits include international training, business scale-up support, and product development. Nelieca's focused categories include school students, graduates, and professionals.

Our Project

Nelieca is a dynamic education and media app connecting learners, educators, professionals, and entrepreneurs worldwide. It promotes learning, research, and networking in a spam-free environment through advanced verification. By integrating education and collaboration, Nelieca fosters genuine connections, empowering users to grow academically and professionally across global boundaries.

Course Journey

Course of our journey Our journey's monumental chapters reflect our dedication: The prestigious unveiling of Nelieca's logo by Dr. Shajulin Benedict, the insightful Nelieca Talks series exploring tech trends, and the grand launch of our website by Sujatha Madhav Chandran. Each event marks our commitment to innovation, education, and empowering aspiring minds, guiding us forward on an inspiring path of transformative growth and learning.

Nelieca Talk Series

Nelieca Talks, a 100-day learning series, offers students and entrepreneurs an invaluable opportunity to delve into technology trends, research, and development. Led by industry leaders and top educationalists, this series transcends storytelling, guiding participants toward success through practical insights and transformative knowledge crucial for navigating the dynamic tech landscape.

Website Launch

Nelieca's official website was unveiled during Kerala Innovation Week at Kerala startup Mission on May 27th, 2022, by Sujatha Madhav Chandran, Head of Competency Development, Data Services of Analytics and Insights Unit at Tata Consultancy Services. This momentous launch marked a significant step forward, solidifying our commitment to empowering education and innovation.
